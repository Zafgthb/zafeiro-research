Privacy Policy – Zafeiro Research

Last updated: 2025-10-07

Overview

This Policy explains how Zafeiro Research processes public TikTok comments and metadata for academic research purposes only.

Data We Process

  • Comment-level data and metadata: comment_id, video_id, comment text, create_time, like_count, reply_count.
  • No collection of private or sensitive data. Where feasible, user identifiers are pseudonymized.

Legal Basis & Compliance

Processing is carried out for academic research in the public/legitimate interest and in compliance with TikTok’s terms and applicable data-protection laws (e.g., GDPR).

How We Use the Data

To study discourse dynamics (volume, persistence, sentiment/emotions) related to brand crises. Results are reported only in aggregate and anonymized form.

Data Minimization

We collect only what is necessary for the stated research aims and avoid storing full user profiles.

Storage & Retention

Data are retained for up to 12 months and then deleted or irreversibly anonymized, unless a longer period is required by law or ethics approval.

Security

We apply technical and organizational measures, including encryption at rest, access restricted to the research team, and access logging.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with collaborators under academic agreements requiring confidentiality and compliance with this Policy and TikTok’s terms. Information may be disclosed if required by law.

International Transfers

If data are transferred across borders, appropriate safeguards will be applied consistent with applicable data-protection laws.

Your Rights

Requests regarding TikTok account data (access, deletion, etc.) should be directed to TikTok. For questions about this project or this Policy, contact [email@university.xx].

Children

This research does not target or intentionally process data specifically about children.
